# Buy My Mixtape Fam

Just create a little store front for my website. I have been wanting to do this for years. Years, I tell you. The idea is that people should be able to connect their wallet to a website, pay for the product (in this case, Randhawa EP, a two track mixtape), and be on their way. No banks involved, no 3rd parties, pure peer-to-peer commerce. Love it.
