# Buy My Mixtape Fam

Just created a little store front for my website. I have been wanting to do this for years, years I tell you. The idea is that people should be able to connect their wallet to a website, pay for the product (in this case, Randhawa EP, a two track mixtape), and be on their way. No banks involved, no 3rd parties, pure peer-to-peer commerce. Love it.

I also added a feature to email yourself the links to the files that last 24 hours.
