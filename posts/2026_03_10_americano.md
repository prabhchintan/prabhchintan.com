# Americano

I keep realizing the genius of an Americano the older I get. When I moved to America, my first job as a teenager was at a Walmart in Houston. It’s where I first saw the trademark American drip coffee. It neither registered nor made much sense.

Then when I worked at an oil refinery a few years later, I saw again people drinking loads of black coffee there.

Black coffee is the American worker’s coffee.

An “Americano” is watered down black coffee.

The gentleman-laborer’s drink.
