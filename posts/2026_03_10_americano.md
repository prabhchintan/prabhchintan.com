# Americano

I keep realizing the genius of an Americano the older I get. When I moved to America as a teenager, my first job was at a Walmart in Houston. It’s where I first saw the trademark American drip coffee. It neither registered nor made much sense.

Then I worked at an oil refinery a few years later, and I saw again people drinking loads of black coffee there. It's where I realized that drip coffee is the American worker’s coffee.

An “Americano” is watered down black coffee. The gentleman-laborer’s drink.
