# Spam Welcome

This blog now includes a comments section. But not just any comments section. One has to pay 1 USDC to write a comment. In one way that sounds ridiculous, but the idea is to have a cost attached to actions that in large numbers may prove to be junk or spam. This simple rule, while potentially unpopular, has many benefits, two of my favorites being: I don't have to moderate comments, and I get to gain from any potential spam.

"Pricing via Processing (or Combatting Junk Mail)" was a pivotal paper written in 1992 by Cynthia Dwork and Moni Naor that proved to be an intellectual ancestor to blockchains. The idea was "a computational technique for combatting junk mail in particular and controlling access to a shared resource in general."

https://www.wisdom.weizmann.ac.il/~naor/PAPERS/pvp.pdf

Brilliant. All spam welcome.
